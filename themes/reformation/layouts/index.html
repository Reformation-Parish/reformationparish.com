Our Worship

The Marks of a
Faithful Parish

I

The Word

The Holy Bible is God's inspired, inerrant revelation to man — the only certain, sufficient, and infallible rule for faith and life. Every Lord's Day we sit under the expository preaching of God's Word.

II

The Sacraments

The Lord's Supper is a covenant meal in which Christ invites us to His table and renews us with bread and wine, observed each Lord's Day. All baptized covenant members — including baptized children who are able to eat and drink — are welcome at the Table.

III

Prayer

Our liturgy is an ordered ascent in prayer — confession, absolution, intercession, and thanksgiving — the covenant people coming before their God with one voice.
